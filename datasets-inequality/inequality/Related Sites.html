Several other federal agencies publish data on income inequality.

Summary statistics based on a sample of individual income tax returns.

This interactive application makes it easier to access and use BEA national, international, regional and industry statistics.

This report includes integrated data from the Diary and Interview portions of the Consumer Expenditure Survey (CE) including one-page data tables.

CBO regularly analyzes the distribution of household income before and after government transfers and federal taxes.

Joint Economic Committee: Income Inequality in the United States

This report examines trends regarding poverty, the middle class, income inequality and economic mobility in the United States.

U.S. Department of Commerce: Middle Class in America

This report examines how two-parent two-child families and single-parent two-child families at different income levels might achieve a middle class lifestyle.

MAY 2015

Bureau of Labor Statistics: A look at pay at the top, the bottom, and in between

This Spotlight on Statistics looks at measures of earnings and wages and examines how these measures have changed over time.

World Bank

Free and open access to data about development in countries around the globe.
